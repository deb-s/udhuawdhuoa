Mathayom 3, or Grade 10 Chonradsadornumrung school, students are enrolled in a comprehensive and multidisciplinary program that integrates various fields of study. The curriculum is designed to provide a well-rounded education, encompassing subjects such as science, mathematics, Thai language, computer studies, and 3 multiple language. This broad approach ensures that students receive a balanced education while also exploring their individual interests and strengths. Students are encouraged to participate in a range of competitions that align with their academic and extracurricular interests. These competitions serve as an opportunity for students to apply their knowledge and skills in practical settings, fostering both personal and academic growth. The skills required for these competitions span a diverse range, including drama and skit performance, scientific inquiry, mathematical problem-solving, creative writing, storytelling, and other multifaceted abilities. By engaging in these activities, students not only enhance their expertise in specific areas but also develop critical thinking, teamwork, and communication skills. The aim is to cultivate a holistic educational experience that prepares students for future academic challenges and personal endeavors. Overall, the Mathayom 3 curriculum is structured to support students in their academic journey while also encouraging them to explore and excel in various fields through practical application and competition
